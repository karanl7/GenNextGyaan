    Celebrating India's Rich Heritage of Sports and Yoga

    India is a country rich in history, culture, and tradition and a diversified sporting past that dates back centuries. Traditional games and yoga have long been crucial to the country's social and cultural fabric.
    These exercises are beyond just sports; they are expressions of the Indian character, demonstrating their strength of body, quickness of mind, and natural ties to their ancestors.

    YOGA

    YOGA is an ancient practice that originated in India. It combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation for health and well-being.
    The world has recently seen an unusual growth in the uptake of yoga.

    CHESS

    CHESS was born in India during the Gupta dynasty in the 6th century. Today, more than 1500 years later, it is played in 172 countries.
    Chess is one of India's contribution to world culture, with games played in the court of kings and now, is a professional sport.

    LUDO

    LUDO, also known as Pachisi is an Indian creation and was invented during ancient times.
    The earliest proof can be witnessed during the era of the Mahabharata

    Kabaddi

    KABADDI, also known as kaudi is a contact team sport played between two teams of seven players, originating from India.
    The history of kabaddi can be traced back to some 4,000 years ago in India.

    Silambam

    SILAMBAM is an ancient weapon-based martial art that emerged in Tamilakam, which is now Tamil Nadu region of India.
    It is one of the oldest martial arts in the world.

    kho_kho

    Experts believe that KHO KHO originated in the Maharashtra region of India and in the ancient times was played on chariots and was called Rathera.
    Rath is the Hindi translation for a chariot.
