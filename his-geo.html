    States

    MAHARASHTRA, one of India's largest commercial and industrial centres, also being called the gateway of India. It is famous for its culture and beauty which makes it a popular tourist destinations.

    GOA in India, is famous for its beaches and places of worship. Tourism is its primary industry, and is generally focused on the coastal areas of Goa, with decreased tourist activity inland.

    KARNATAKA, in southern India, is a diverse state with a mix of culture, history, and natural beauty and ancient heritages like Hampi's ruins. It has lovely nature spots too, which tourists enjoy exploring.

    GUJARAT, home state of Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation. It is renowned for its beaches, temple towns and historic capitals. Sculpture, handicrafts, arts, festivals add to the richness of state.

    NEW DELHI, is best known as the location of India's national government. It has great historical significance as it was home to powerful people, such as the Pāṇḍavas and the Mughals.

    KERALA, is a popular destination for both domestic as well as foreign tourists. Kerala is well known for its beaches, backwaters in Alappuzha and Kollam, mountain ranges and wildlife sanctuaries.
